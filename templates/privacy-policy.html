Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how InstaFit handles your data.

Last Updated: August 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to InstaFit ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Photos You Upload

When you use InstaFit to try on clothes virtually, you may upload photos of yourself. Here's how we handle this data:

  • No Permanent Storage: We do not store your photos on our servers
  • Temporary Processing: Photos are sent to Google's Gemini API for AI processing to generate virtual try-on results
  • Local Storage Only: Photos are temporarily stored locally on your device for the duration of your session
  • Automatic Deletion: Local photo data is automatically cleared after 24 hours

2.2 Product Information

We collect information about products you're viewing on e-commerce websites to provide the virtual try-on service:

  • Product images from supported e-commerce sites
  • Product titles and basic information
  • Website URLs for product pages

2.3 Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service:

  • Which e-commerce sites are most frequently used
  • Feature usage patterns
  • Performance metrics

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Purpose

Your photos and product information are used solely to provide the virtual try-on service through AI processing.

3.2 Service Improvement

Anonymous usage data helps us improve InstaFit's functionality and user experience.

3.3 Future Features

We may introduce affiliate links in the future. If implemented, these would be clearly marked and would not involve sharing your personal data with third parties.

🔒 Important: We Do Not

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Use your photos for any purpose other than virtual try-on
  • Share your photos with advertisers or marketing companies
  • Require you to create an account or provide personal information

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Gemini API

We use Google's Gemini API to process your photos and generate virtual try-on results. Your photos are sent to Google's servers for processing but are not stored by Google for our use.

4.2 E-commerce Websites

InstaFit works with various e-commerce websites. We only access publicly available product information from these sites.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Secure transmission of data to third-party APIs
  • Local storage encryption where applicable
  • Automatic data cleanup to minimize data retention

6. Data Retention

Our data retention practices are designed to minimize data storage:

  • Photos: Not stored on our servers; local data deleted after 24 hours
  • Usage Data: Anonymous statistics may be retained for service improvement
  • Product Information: Not stored; accessed in real-time only

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view what data is stored locally on your device
  • Deletion: Clear all data using the "Clear All Data" button in the extension
  • Control: Choose not to upload photos or use the service

8. Children's Privacy

InstaFit is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

10. International Users

InstaFit is available worldwide. By using our service, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your residence, including the United States, where our servers and third-party services may be located.
